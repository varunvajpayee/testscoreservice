B) RISK TYPES

 

1.     Financial Risks

 

a.       Market Risk - Risk of decline in portfolio value due to change in market risk factors.

b.       Credit Risk -  Risk of counterparty defaulting (partially paying) in his/her obligation.

c.       Liquidity Risk - Risk of fall in liquidity of the business to operate efficiently.

 

2.     Non-Financial Risks

 

a.       Operational risk - loss due to failure of the company's system and procedures or from external events outside the company's control.

b.       Settlement risk (Herstatt risk)- In a swap transaction, Herstatt bank received the payment but defaulted on making the payment. So, it's a risk involved at the time of settlement of contract.

c.       Model risk- Risk related to incorrect model specification.

d.       Sovereign (political) risk -  Risk of foreign government default(decline) in making the payments of debts.

e.       Regulatory risk- Risk related to unfavorable regulation, fees or fines imposed by regulators.

f.        Tax risk - Risk of rise in effective corporate taxes.

g.       Accounting risk -  Uncertainty about how a transaction are recorded and the potential for accounting rules (Like GAAP) and regulations to change.

h.       Legal(contract) risk - risk arising from failure to comply with statutory or regulatory obligations.

 

 ESG risk= E= Environment Damage risk, S=Social=> Human resource issues, G=Governance=> Corporate Governance.
