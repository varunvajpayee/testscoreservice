III.                  DUTIES TO CLIENTS

 

A.      Loyalty, Prudence, and Care


1.  Soft dollar should benefit client.
2. Always seek best to execute for your client trades.
3. Client needs and circumstances should be reviewed at least annually.

 

B.      Fair Dealing (Impartial in your analysis and recommendation)

1. No impartial treatment to any client. Including your family members.
2. Different service levels are ok, provided they are properly disclosed.
3. Fair chance must be given to the clients to act upon the recommendations.
4. Adhoc basis is not good for trade allocation. Trade allocation should be pro rata basis however minimum trade quota can be specified.

 

C.      Suitability

 

1. As per investment policy statement.
2. Investment should be in context of entire portfolio (not just on current transactions).

 

D.     Performance Presentation

1. Fair, Accurate, and complete.
2. Present the performance of a composite as a weighted average of the performance rather than using a single representative account (survivorship bias).
3. this standard does not prohibit showing past performance of funds managed at a prior firm as part of a performance record of accomplishment, if showing that record is accompanied by appropriate disclosures about where the performance took place and the person’s specific role in achieving the performances.

4.Read GIPS for more detail.

 

E.      Preservation of Confidentiality

 

1. Including current, former, and prospective clients.

2.Providing information to CFA institute investigation is not considered a violation.

3. Confidentiality should be maintained, unless required by the law, Illegal activity or client allows that.
