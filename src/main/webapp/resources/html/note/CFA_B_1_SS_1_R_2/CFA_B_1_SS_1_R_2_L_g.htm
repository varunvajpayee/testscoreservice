VII.                  RESPONSIBILITIES AS A CFA INSTITUTE MEMBER OR CFA CANDIDATE

                              A.            Conduct as Participants in CFA Institute Programs

 

1.       Mostly related with exam- No cheating permitted, Even you are not even allowed to discuss the weights of the sections in the exam. You can still discuss practice tests.

2.       You can still express your opinions, beliefs concerning CFA institute.

 

B.      Reference to CFA Institute, the CFA Designation, and the CFA Program

 

1.       You can use it after the name or as an adjective.

2.       You can say, you passed CFA exams in three consecutive years but you cannot say being CFA, you are superior investment manager.

3.       Sign PCS annually and pay CFA dues every year to be an active CFA chart holder.

4.       You can put CFA logo on your personal letterhead, business card (only if you are a chart holder (Not a candidate)) but cannot put on company's letterhead.

5.       Sign with oversize bold strike CFA letter is also not permitted. (Exaggerate).

6.       CFA Program fee would be considered a personal and firm's expense. It should not be paid from client's expense (brokerage commission or soft dollars). Soft dollars are intended to help client only.

7.       All members and candidates must comply with CFA inquiries even though it involves disclosing confidential information about the client. 

8.       I am a CFA (noun) NOT Allowed. However, I am a CFA charter holder is permissible. 

9.       Firms are encouraged to inform CFA Institute of its claim but it is not mandatory. (to compliant with CFA code and the conducts).
