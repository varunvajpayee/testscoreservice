II.                  INTEGRITY OF CAPITAL MARKETS

 

A.      Material Nonpublic Information

1. failure to prevent the transfer of MNI is equally bad. You should not act or cause other to act on MNI.
2. Acting on some public information and with some items of nonpublic NON-material information allowed. -mosaic theory
3. Employees in possession of material nonpublic information should make reasonable efforts to achieve public dissemination of the information if such actions would not breach a duty.
4. if you are supervisor and you have MNI then consider adding that security to the firm's restrictive list.
5. Just mere monitoring MNI is not sufficient, there must be physical and information security system between IB and brokerage operation.

 

B.      Market Manipulation

1.if there is a pump priming (common to create artificial liquidity for future contract during starting days) and it's disclosed properly, It won't considered as market manipulation.
