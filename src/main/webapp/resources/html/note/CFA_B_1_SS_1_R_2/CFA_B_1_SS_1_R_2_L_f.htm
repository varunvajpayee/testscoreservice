VI.  CONFLICTS OF INTEREST

 

A.      Disclosure of Conflicts 

1. If something can interfere your objectivity then that should be property disclosed.

2. Your pension fund contribution isn't subject to disclose requirements.

3. If you are holding a position in a company and working for that company in your own time and business of that company is totally different from your employer and will not impact your employer and client then you DON'T have to disclose your relationship with the company and the compensation to your employer or client. 

 

B.      Priority of Transactions

1. Family members accounts with firm are like any client's account.

2. Must give clients adequate time (say 1 week) to act upon the information before member start using that information for its purpose (own account trading). 

3. Priority of Transactions does not limit transactions of company employees that differ from current recommendations as long as the sale does not disadvantage current clients.

 

C.      Referral Fees

1. Candidates must disclose to their employer, clients, and prospective clients, as appropriate, any compensation, consideration, or benefit received from or paid to others for the recommendation of products or services.
