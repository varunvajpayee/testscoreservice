Investors buy a low strike price calls (XL) but you sell higher strike price calls (XH) so that they can cover some cost.

Expectation = Price will increase above XL but will not increase more than XH.

Expectation: The underlying will trade near the middle exercise price.

5.     Straddle

Investor buy both put and call options on same underlying with same strike price.

Expectation =Investor expect price to change in either direction. Investor lose money when price do not change. Price should at least change so that they can cover up their both options cost.

 

Scenarios

Value

Profit

0

Maximum Profit

St-X-C-P

(Assuming St can go up unlimited)

Maximum loss

-(-C-P)=C+P

 

Breakeven

X+C+P
