A) RISK MANAGEMENT APPLICATIONS OF OPTIONS STRATEGIES-I

 

Terms-

St = Stock Price at time t

X = Strike Price

C0 = Call Price

P0 = Put Price

XL = Low Strike Price

XH= High Strike Price

CH =Call Price corresponding to higher strike price.

CL =Call Price corresponding to lower strike price.

PH =Put Price corresponding to higher strike price.

PL =Put Price corresponding to lower strike price.

 

1.     Call Option

Investors make money when stock price go more than the strike price.

Expectation = Price will increase.

4.     Covered Call

Investors make money by writing the calls but let go the upside potential of the underlying, which they own.

Expectation =Price will stay in where they are, (low volatile).

Scenarios

Profit

Breakeven

S0-C0
