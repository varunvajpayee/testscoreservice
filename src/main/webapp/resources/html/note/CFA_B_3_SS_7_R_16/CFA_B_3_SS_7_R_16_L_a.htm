2.     Gordon Growth Model for developed Market

Since the growth is stable in developed economies So GGM is an appropriate model value such markets.

 

3.     H Model for developing economies

Since H model assumes two different growth rates, a period of declining growth follows a period of higher growth. Due to this flexibility to support multiple growth rates, it is more suitable to value developing economies.

 

 

4.     Top down Vs Bottom up forecasting approach

 

A) Top down analysis

·         Based on macroeconomic variables but slow to reflect the structural changes in the economy.

·         It starts from Market analysis to Industry analysis and go further to company level analysis.

 

B) Bottom-up approach

·         Forecast starts by looking at fundamentals of a company.

·         Individual company level analysis could be aggregated up to sector, industry, or asset class level.
