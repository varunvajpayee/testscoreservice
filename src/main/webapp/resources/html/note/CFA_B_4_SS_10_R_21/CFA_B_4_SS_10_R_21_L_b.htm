Trading Constraints in Secondary Market

 

1.      Portfolio Constraints

Like holding period, quality restrictions, floating rate requirements.

2.      “Story” Disagreement

Between sell side and buy side analyst related to trade.

3.       Buy-and-Hold

Like Regret aversion bias, unwillingness to sell and holding a loss position, desire to keep turnover low.

4.      Seasonality

Slow trading at the end of the month.
