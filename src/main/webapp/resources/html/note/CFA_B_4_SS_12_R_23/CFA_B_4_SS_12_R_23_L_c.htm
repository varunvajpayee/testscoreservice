2.     Equity Future

 They are very liquid and are used in managing the exposure of market Factors (like Index return). They have finite life, which is a big disadvantage relative to the direct investment in equity.

 

3.     Equity total return swap

Like Equity future, this can be used in to gain or reduce expose of major indices. Typically, In Equity swap, investor exchanges the return of equity with return of an Index (or return from Fixed Income instrument). 

 

·         Due to their cheaper cost, they are preferred for tactical asset allocation in portfolio rebalancing.

 

·         There are tax advantages of using equity swap than buying the underlying securities directly.
