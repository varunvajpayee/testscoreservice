Characteristics

 

1.       Diversification benefits: They have low correlation with other asset classes (Typically Bond and stocks).

2.       Low liquidity: calls for higher liquidity premium and therefore higher return.

3.       High due diligence costs: Cost associated to research and monitoring.

4.       Difficult to evaluate performance: It's difficult to identify the proper benchmark.

 

Return Characteristics

 

The two characteristics of the returns distribution associated with alternative investment strategies that reduce downside risk are

 

1.       Positive Skewness.

2.       Low Kurtosis.

 

 

Alternative Investment Due Diligence Process

 

1.       Study the Market Opportunity

2.       Study the investment process

3.       Study the organization and its operations(Reseach,trading, Risk Management)

4.       Study the people

5.       Evaluate the terms and structure of the investment

6.       Verify the firm’s service providers

7.       Review Documents.

8.       Write up an action plan before taking any investment decision.

 

AI Issues for private Wealth clients

 

1.       Taxes: Complicated tax structure (Limited partnership) relative to individual taxes.

2.       Suitability: Not suitable for everybody due to lock up period.

3.       Communication: Due to complexity of investment, communication is not easy.

4.       Decision Risk: Risk of emotionally selling the investment at maximum loss.

5.       Concentrated Portfolio: Generally AI as in form of concentrated asset.
