2.    Buyout Funds

 

·         Generally, Buyout funds are highly leveraged and use these funds to in buying established companies.

·         Middle Market funds concentrate on divisions spun off from large public traded company.

·         Large cap buyout fund concentrate on large companies.

·         Generally, add value by restructuring operations and management or buying companies less than their intrinsic value.
