D) GIPS ADVERTISING GUIDELINE

 

1.      All advertisements that state a claim of compliance – must include the definition of the firm & info about how to obtain a compliant presentation and/or a list of the firm’s composite descriptions

 

2.      “[Name of Firm] claims compliance with the Global Investment Performance Standards (GIPS)”

 

3.      Advertisement (a claim of compliance + performance presentation) –must disclose

·         The composite description

·         An indication whether performance is shown gross of fee, net of fee or both

·         The currency used to express returns.

 

One of these total returns should be shown:

 

                                       i.            One, three, and five-year annualized composite returns

                                     ii.            Period to date composite returns in addition to a)

                                   iii.            Period to date composite returns in addition to 5 years of annual composite returns calculated through the same period of time as presented.
