A) GIPS CHARACTERISTICS

 

1.     Why adopt GIPS? (Basic Objectives)

 

a.       Accuracy and transparency in performance presentation to clients.

b.       Self-Regulation.

c.       Helps in comparing the performance trend.

d.       Fair competition at global level.

 

 

2.     GIPS Compliance

It's a Voluntary and set of minimum standards for investment firms to claim the compliance. Compliance must be on a firm wide basis, no partial compliance.  In case GIPS vs Local law conflicts, follow local law and disclose. Only investment management firms may claim compliance with the GIPS standards.

 

To claim the compliance

 

a.       Firm's performance measurement data inputs, process and return calculation as per the GIPS guidelines.

b.       all firm's fee paying discretionary portfolio must be assigned to at least one composite.

c.       Firms need to produce a GIPS compliant report every 12 months for clients.

d.       A firm must present at least 5 years of GIPS compliant data (or since inception if <5 years old) and must then add annual performance each year up to 10 years’ minimum to meet GIPS standards. (Non-compliant GIPS data before year 2000 can be included).

 

 

Total firm assets = fair value of all discretionary assets + nondiscretionary assets

 

3.     Definition of the firm

An investment firm, subsidiary, or division held out to clients as a distinct business entity, which is defined as below.

 

A distinct business entity

 

a.       Functionally/ organizationally separate

b.       Retains discretion over assets

c.       Autonomy over the decision-making process
